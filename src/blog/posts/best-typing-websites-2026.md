## FAQ: Typing Website Questions

### Which typing website is most accurate?

**All major sites are accurate.** Typex, Monkeytype, Keybr, and TypeRacer all use standard WPM calculations. Minor variations come from:
- Word list differences
- Error counting methods
- Test duration variations

Typex uses the industry-standard formula: (Characters ÷ 5) ÷ Minutes = WPM

### Is Typex better than Monkeytype?

**They're different, not necessarily better.**

- **Typex wins on**: Speed, simplicity, no friction
- **Monkeytype wins on**: Customization, features, stats

Choose based on your priorities.

### Do I need to pay for typing websites?

**No — all major typing tests are free.** Some offer premium features:

Typex, Monkeytype, and Keybr are completely free.

### Can I use typing websites on mobile?
